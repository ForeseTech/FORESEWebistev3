Tips to Crack Interview

Interviews are a vital component in the hiring process. With each job interview, we meet new people, we display our skills, stay upbeat and remain enthusiastic through it all. This can be a challenge, especially when you’re interviewing for a job you would love to get hired for!! Glance through these tips to get through almost any interview and make the process look sublime.

  • Research about the company

    Do significant research about the company you are planning to interview for before even applying. Before the interview, knowing about the foundation of the company, their work over the years, economy, perks, average salaries and the achievements surely adds to your preparation for the interview.

  • Reach early to the location

    Punctuality is very important for the interview. Being timely are a few things you must practice in the standard of living because it can always help you. Reach ten to fifteen minutes early so that you can relax and be ready to give the interview.

  • Be confident in your body language

    Win them over with your authenticity and positivity. A confident candidate is always preferred even if the other candidate had a better-looking resume. Respond truthfully to all the questions asked. And do not be ashamed if you do not know an answer.

  • Tie your answers to your skills and accomplishments

    When you’re answering the interview questions, make sure that every answer of yours exhibits a skill or a quality which is tailored perfectly for the job requirements. The goal is that every answer must reflect one unique quality of yours.

Decorum to be maintained during
Mock Placements

  1. Students must be present at their allotted timings, delaying or rescheduling the interview will not be entertained.
  2. Make sure you have a good internet connection.
  3. Make sure your camera is turned on and the mic is working.
  4. There must be no background noises.
  5. Greet the HR once you join the meeting.
  6. Be very respectful and polite to the HR.
  7. Students must login only through their laptops and not through any other devices.
  8. Students must login through their SVCE mail id and not through their personal mail ids.
  9. PDF version of your resume must be kept ready for screen sharing during the interview.
  10. The resume should not exceed one page, follow the resume format given and it should have credible information.
  11. Only students who have attended the GD and aptitude test will be allowed to sit for the Mock Placements.
  12. Make sure you listen to the instructions given by your student in charge.
  13. Do not use colloquial language while talking to the HR.

Tips to make resume

A professionally designed resume is a gateway to your career. Resume building is an art and science. Use these attention grabbing strategies to make the recruiter pick your resume (you only have ten seconds to stand a chance in the eyes of the HR)



  • Pitch your profile


    Provide a professional photo in a formal/semi formal attire with a smile. Use Key terms and meta data so that your resume is discoverable by not just HR but also AI bots

  • Highlight key strengths


    Prioritize highlighting your most significant accomplishments and credentials. Include any electives and specialties in addition to your main course of study.

  • Keep it personal and professional


    Your resume should reflect your personality and stay unique as well as present professional achievements and include distinctive hobbies.

  • Other Professional work


    Include any projects you've worked on, as well as any seminars, publications, or conferences you've attended.

  • Go beyond academics


    Show that you are a multifaceted individual by listing your club and sport activities in addition to your academic achievements.

  • Establish social media presence


    Build a LinkedIn page and get connected with professionals, as well as being active on Instagram and Twitter to remain updated.
